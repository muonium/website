HELP

I lost my password, how can I recover it ?

If this is your identification password, then click on "Forgot password?" on the login page. Your password will be reset and an email will be sent with a temporary password. However, if this is your decryption password, it's impossible for us to redefine one and you must consider that all your files are lost.

Who can access my files ?

Only you can access your files. Without technical terms, you need a key (the "passphrase") to encrypt and decrypt your files. Thus, nobody, not even we, can access your files, you apart.

How my files are encrypted ?

our files are encrypted end-to-end thanks to SJCL at your browser using your personal key, also decrypted in your browser. Thus, data that are sent are already encrypted and nobody else except you can decrypt them.

Are you trying to help terrorists or evil organizations ?

No, we advocate privacy, certainly helping the world get a better privacy, we unwittingly helping terrorism. But we're in the same situation as ProtonMail. In any case, even if we wouldn't develop Muonium, terrorists and any other person or group of people will encrypt their data.
